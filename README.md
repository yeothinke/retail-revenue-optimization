# Retail Revenue Optimization Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes retail transaction data from the Sample Superstore Dataset to identify key drivers of revenue and profitability. The objective is to understand customer purchasing behaviour, evaluate the effectiveness of discount strategies, and generate insights that support revenue growth while maintaining healthy profit margins.

The analysis focuses on three key questions:
- How concentrated is revenue among customers?
- Do discounts actually improve business performance?
- What discount levels balance sales growth and profitability?

The results highlight the trade-off between promotional strategies and profitability, a common challenge faced by retail and consumer goods companies.
